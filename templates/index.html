Artificial Intelligence

The undergraduate-level degree programme in Artificial Intelligence (AI) provides students with a thorough knowledge of AI and techniques. Students typically study Artificial Intelligence course syllabus such as Introduction to AI, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, and Robotics.

Anatomy

Anatomy is a three years undergraduate degree course that deals with the study of tissues, structures, organs of humans. The course is termed to be a specialized medicine branch. Some of the job designations are Medical Coder, Team Leader, Configuration Analyst, Lecturer, And Assistant Professor.

Phd in Computer Science

The PhD Computer Science duration is three years, which can be maximum extended to 5 years. PhD Computer Science Admission 2023 requires students to have a minimum of 55% in postgraduate degree and should qualify for the entrance exams such as UGC-NET, RET, DET, etc, as an additional criterion by colleges.
